Split Pea Soupe

Description

his hearty split pea soup with ham has a slightly sweet, somewhat salty, and subtle smoky flavor. An ideal fall or winter soup â€” great for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 ribs celery, diced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 pound dried spit peas, rinsed
  • 1 pound ham, diced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step1

    Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium-low heat. Stir in celery, onion, and sliced garlic; cook and stir until onions are translucent but not brown, 5 to 8 minutes.

  2. Step2

    KStir in split peas, ham, and bay leaf. Pour in chicken stock and water; stir to combine and simmer until peas are tender and soup has thickened, about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Stir occasionally. Season with salt and black pepper to serve.
